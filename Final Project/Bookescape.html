Featured Author of the Month!

Colleen Hoover

Colleen Hoover's best seller "It Ends with Us" that was published on 2016 will be going to big screen!
Starring Blake Lively as Lily, Justin Baldoni as Ryle and Brandon Sklenar as Atlas. It is directed by Justin Baldoni and produced by Columbia Pictures in association with Wayfarer Studios and Saks Pictures. It Ends with Us is scheduled to be released in the United States on August 9, 2024, by Sony Pictures Releasing.
The film will follow Lily, a woman who moves to a new city following college graduation and meets and falls in love with a man named Ryle. Her newfound bliss isn't long-lived, however, as a blast from the past comes back in the form of her ex-lover who turns her world upside down.

The official trailer for the movie was finally released on May 16, 2024 and it's set to none other than Blake's bestie Taylor Swift's song "My Tears Ricochet."
